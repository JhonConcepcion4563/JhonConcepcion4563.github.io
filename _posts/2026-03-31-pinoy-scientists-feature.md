Pinoy Scientists featured my journey as a Filipino scientist — from studying mutant rice lines at Central Luzon State University, to working at PhilRice, and now conducting wheat breeding research at Michigan State University.

I was featured on [Pinoy Scientists](https://pinoyscientists.com/scientist/jonathan-concepcion/), a platform that highlights Filipino scientists making an impact in their fields.

The feature covers my research journey — from my undergraduate work on mutant rice lines at Central Luzon State University in the Philippines, to my work as a Science Research Analyst at the Philippine Rice Research Institute, and now my research at Michigan State University focusing on wheat breeding, Fusarium Head Blight resistance, and the integration of phenomics and genomics to accelerate breeding pipelines.

Check out the full feature here: [https://pinoyscientists.com/scientist/jonathan-concepcion/](https://pinoyscientists.com/scientist/jonathan-concepcion/)
