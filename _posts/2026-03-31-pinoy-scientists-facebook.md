Pinoy Scientists shared my profile on their Facebook page, reaching the broader Filipino science community and highlighting my work in plant breeding and genetics.

[Pinoy Scientists](https://www.facebook.com/pinoyscientists) is a Facebook community and online platform that shares stories and highlights the work and everyday lives of Filipino scientists from around the world. Founded by Filipina astrophysicist Reina Reyes, the page aims to break stereotypes about what scientists look like and inspire more Filipinos by spotlighting scientists in various fields.
